## Overview

Fidelity.UI will be available under a Commercial License for use in commercial products, services, and enterprise environments. The Commercial License provides rights and protections beyond those offered by the Apache License 2.0, and enables access to Professional tier features.

## Fidelity.UI Editions

Fidelity.UI will be available in two editions:

### Community Edition (Apache 2.0)

The Community Edition is free and open source under the Apache License 2.0. It includes:

- Signal-based reactive primitives
- Component composition DSL
- Layout engine (stack, flex, grid)
- Platform rendering backends (Wayland, Quartz, Win32)
- Design token system and theming
- Headless component primitives (Dialog, Select, Menu, Tabs)
- Accessibility foundations

Suitable for:
- Individual developers
- Open source projects
- Educational use
- Evaluation and learning

### Professional Edition (Commercial License)

The Professional Edition requires a Commercial License and includes everything in Community plus:

- **Advanced Layout**: Constraint-based layout solver, responsive breakpoint system
- **Animation System**: Spring physics, gesture-driven transitions, timeline orchestration
- **Component Library**: Pre-styled component sets with multiple design system themes
- **Performance Profiling**: Signal dependency visualization, render region heatmaps
- **Design Tools**: Token extraction from Figma, live theme editing
- **Priority Support**: Direct access to the development team
- **Commercial Use**: Rights for commercial products and services
